We are a student-faculty cooperative driven by research, development, and an enthusiasm for social impact. Based at the Interactive Media program at the University of Miami School of Communication, we function as an incubator for initiatives that collaborate locally, nationally, and in the global space. NERDLab integrates classic and novel techniques to produce projects that inspire and educate through innovation and fun. See some of our work here.
